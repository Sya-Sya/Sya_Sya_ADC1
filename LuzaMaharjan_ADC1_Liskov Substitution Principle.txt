SOLID Principles is a coding standard that all developers should have a clear concept for developing software in a proper way to avoid a bad design. 
It was promoted by Robert C Martin and is used across the object-oriented design spectrum. When applied properly it makes your code more extendable,
logical and easier to read.

Liskov Substitution Principle :

The Liskov Substitution principle was introduced by Barbara Liskov in her conference keynote “Data abstraction” in 1987.
According to Liskov Substitution principle “Derived classes must be substitutable for their base classes".
